Anna Matsumoto

ようこそ、私の世界へ。

今、あなたはどんな世界を見ていますか？

  • STRANGER

    • "STRANGER" was published on August 7, 2018, at my age of 15.

    I named the man "STRANGER" who suddenly began to appear in my dreams every single day.

  • NOZOKU

    • I drew it on the black board

    and expresses that we can't escape from someone's sight.

  • OZONE HOLE

    • You can see this at Shibuya, Tokyo.

  • PSYCHE

    • Rainbow braces, two tongues, bloodshot eyes that suggest sleep deprivation,

    and all sorts of things pouring out of her head prove that this is a representation of who I am.

  • PSYENCE

    • It was inspired by the term "psyence" produced by Hideto Matsumoto

    which means the fusion of psycho and science.

    As you can see, his arms and legs are made of machines,

    his heart has been gouged out, and he is forced to smile at the seams.

    In other words, technology, which is supposed to create happiness, can also destroy it.
